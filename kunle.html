Cruchy Fried Shrimp

These fried shrimp have just the right amount of crunch thanks to the panko bread crumbs. If desired, serve with Kikkoman Tonkatsu Sauce for dipping. Restaurant-worthy fried shrimp is 15 minutes (and a few simple ingredients) away. Take it from us: This irresistibly crunchy fried shrimp recipe is impossible to beat.

Fried Shrimp Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to make the best fried shrimp of your life:

  • Flour: Dredging your shrimp in all-purpose flour before frying promotes a wonderfully crunchy crust.
  • Seasonings: This fried shrimp is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.
  • Eggs: Dipping the floured shrimp in eggs gives the bread crumbs something to adhere to.
  • Bread crumbs: Panko bread crumbs add the perfect amount of flavor and crunch.
  • Shrimp: You'll need one pound of peeled, deveined jumbo shrimp.
  • Oil: Vegetable oil is great for frying, as it has a neutral flavor and can stand up to high heat.

How to fry shrimp

    Step 1
  • Mix flour, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. Beat eggs in a second medium bowl until frothy. Place bread crumbs in a third bowl.
    • Step 2
  • Dredge shrimp in the flour mixture, then shake off excess. Dip shrimp into beaten eggs, then press shrimp into bread crumbs, turning to coat both sides.
    • Step 3
  • Heat 2 inches oil in a large, heavy pot to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    • Step 4
  • Deep-fry shrimp in batches in the hot oil until cooked through, about 1 minute. Use tongs to transfer shrimp to a paper towel-lined plate to drain. Repeat to cook remaining shrimp.
    • Step 5
  • Arrange shrimp on a platter to serve.
